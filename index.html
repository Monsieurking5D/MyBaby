Romantic Getaways

Musée de la Vie Romantique

9th arrondissement

The ultimate romantic spot. A former artist's house with a hidden garden and a glass conservatory. Perfect for tea.

Le Musée Rodin

7th arrondissement

Majestic and intimate. Stroll among world-famous statues in a magnificent French garden.

Le Petit Palais

8th arrondissement

Sumptuous Belle Époque architecture with a breathtaking exotic central patio — pure aesthetics for a romantic stroll.

La Galerie Dior

8th arrondissement

Ultra cottagecore and spectacular with its famous spiral staircase. A haute couture paradise.

Unique Experiences

L'Atelier des Lumières

11th arrondissement

A must-see. Total immersion among artworks projected floor to ceiling with an epic soundtrack — a poetic, timeless moment.

Musée des Arts Forains

12th arrondissement

The most magical. It feels like stepping into an old movie or Hogwarts. Century-old carousels, distorting mirrors, and a hushed, mysterious atmosphere — deeply romantic and utterly unique (advance booking required).

Le Paradox Museum

9th arrondissement

The most fun. Less serious than an art museum — the perfect place to laugh together, take insane photos (like walking on the ceiling), and break the ice.

Bourse de Commerce

1st arrondissement

The most modern and visually striking. The giant architecture with its immense glass dome creates a stunning wow effect in a gorgeous setting — hugely trendy on TikTok right now.

For the Evening

L'Inavoué

1st arrondissement

A hidden restaurant with sharing plates and excellent cocktails. Perfect for conversation without the noise.

Le Très Particulier

18th arrondissement

A more elegant but still intimate vibe. With its hidden garden, it doesn't get any quieter.

Mobster Bar

11th arrondissement

An immersive spot with 1920s decor. To get in, you have to solve a riddle — which makes the experience even cooler!

L'Arbonne Cocktail Bar

6th arrondissement

A cozy little spot, perfect for a quiet drink and a relaxed conversation.
